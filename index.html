A little about me

Hi, I’m Corné Schipper, and I’m 21 years old. I completed a 3-year MBO Software Developer program at Hoornbeeck College and am currently a second-year student in Communication and Multimedia Design at Avans University of Applied Sciences.

I’m passionate about technology, creativity, and problem-solving. In my free time, I enjoy gaming, skiing, building PCs, and spending time with friends over a beer.

Feel free to explore my portfolio, get in touch, or follow me!
